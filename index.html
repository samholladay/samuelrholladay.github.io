I am a screenwriter and director based out of San Francisco, CA. Originally from New Orleans, LA, I work full-time as an engineer but have always had a passion for film.

My work spans genres from satire to horror, often with a focus on social and class dynamics. I have written three feature-length screenplays, and more than 10 short scripts. I have directed one short film, Never Say You're Sorry (in collaboration with Encounters Film Collective) which is currently being submitted to film festivals.
